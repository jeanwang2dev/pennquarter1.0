The Penn Quarter Neighborhood Association hosts monthly breakfast meetings on timely and relevant topics delivered by interesting speakers. There is no charge for member organizations, residents, and those working in Penn Quarter to attend the meetings to network, hear about new developments, and learn from the different speakers delivering useful information on plans for the Penn Quarter neighborhood.
