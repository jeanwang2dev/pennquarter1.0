Welcome to the Penn Quarter Neighborhood Association

Now over thirty years old, the Penn Quarter Neighborhood Association, PQNA for short, counts 130 members. These include restaurants, shops, commercial office tenants, museums, hotels, theaters, non-profits, public agencies, property owners, and residences.

The PQNA serves its members by promoting the many activities its members sponsor, whether they are museum programs, films, theatrical productions, art exhibitions, wine dinners, book signings, lectures, or musical performances. The PQNA also publicizes meetings informing denizens about public agencies’ plans, and projects that private developers are planning or are in the early stages of construction. In addition, the PQNA hosts monthly breakfast meetings on timely and relevant topics delivered by interesting speakers.

With over a dozen theaters and museums, many restaurants and shops, the Capital One Arena, and offices, hotels, and residences, Penn Quarter became a national, award winning model of a lively and exciting downtown mixed-use neighborhood.

Penn Quarter includes much of Washington, DC’s downtown between the White House and The Capitol. It is bounded by:

  • New York Avenue, NW, Mt. Vernon Square, and Massachusetts Avenue, NW on the north
  • Pennsylvania Avenue, NW on the south
  • 3rd Street, NW on the east, and
  • 15th Street, NW on the west
