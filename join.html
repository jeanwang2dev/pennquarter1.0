Become a member of the Penn Quarter Neighboorhood Association

Becoming a member of the Penn Quarter Neighborhood Association (PQNA) is easy; it brings with it many benefits. Your business, cultural institution, or apartment/condo building will become part of a growing community of business owners, residents, and people who work in the Penn Quarter neighborhood. The PQNA maintains a list of some 1,800 people interested in the neighborhood community and want to know the latest about what’s happening in Penn Quarter.

Benefits include:

  • An announcement that your business/residence/theatre/etc joined the PQNA at the first breakfast meeting following the date you joined.
  • Receipt of a template and instructions on setting up an announcement for the PQNA to send to the 1,800 on its list.
  • General announcements about your business or cultural institution such as new menus, programs, or special offers sent to the neighborhood community.
  • A reasonable annual fee based on the size of buildings owned, the number employed by retail businesses or office tenants, the number working for a non-profit, or the number of residences in a condominium.
  • Advocacy regarding neighborhood issues such as zoning, development, safety, transportation, and livability that matter to businesses, cultural institutions, and residents.
  • Access to making announcements at the Association’s monthly breakfast meetings attended regularly by Penn Quarter business owners, residents, and employees.
